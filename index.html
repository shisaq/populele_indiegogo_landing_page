Handcrafted using premium European maple and Italian Aquila strings, Populele sports an innovative fretboard learning system with 72 built-in LEDS, which connect via Bluetooth to deliver an unparalleled gamified app experience. With its elegant design, Populele provides a learning experience nothing short of revolutionary.

With built-in recording, you can record and share your song on social media with your friends.

Continuously-updated song library includes most popular songs.

1. Do you ship worldwide?

We currently only ship to the US due to battery shipping regulations. More countries will be covered late this year.

2. When will I receive my Populele?

The first 2,000 units can be guaranteed to be shipped out before the end of July. Production will be running in full capacity in late April according to crowd funding performance.

3. What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs change depending on the chosen perk and your location. Select your location in the check out process and the shipping cost will be shown instantly.

4. How long does the Populele last once it's fully charged?

About 10 hours.

5. How to Charge Populele?

Populele can be charged by regular smart phone chargers with USB to Micro USB cable.

6. How much does Populele weigh and what is the size?

Populele weights 0.5 KG (1.1 Lbs). The size is : 24 * 8 * 2-3/4 (inches).

7. What kind of material is Populele made of?

Premium European maple, first class spruce, and Italian Aquila strings.
